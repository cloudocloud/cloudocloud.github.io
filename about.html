About us

Sungwoo Choi

Founder and creative director of cloudocloud. Yejin works across spatial storytelling, editorial design, and cultural research, shaping portfolios and brand narratives with a calm visual rhythm.

After studying visual communication and exhibition-based storytelling, she has developed projects that connect travel observation, place-based identity, and digital presentation. Her practice focuses on translating atmosphere into structures that feel clear, warm, and memorable.
